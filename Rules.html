Library Rules

Follow these rules to keep the library peaceful, safe, and helpful for everyone.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Maintain silence:

    Maintain silence at all times inside the library to respect others who are studying or reading.

  2. Handle with care:

    Handle books and materials gently to preserve them for future readers.

  3. Return on time:

    Return borrowed books on or before the due date; renew if you need more time.

  4. Phones on silent:

    Keep mobile phones on silent mode and take calls outside reading areas.

  5. No food or drinks:

    No food or uncovered drinks are allowed to prevent damage to books and equipment.

  6. For study & research:

    Use library resources responsibly for study, research, and learning only.

  7. Respect staff:

    Respect library staff and follow their instructions for smooth operation.

  8. No marking:

    Do not write, mark, or deface books, labels, or furniture.
